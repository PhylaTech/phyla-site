Open source

Autometa

An open-source pipeline for pulling individual microbial genomes out of shotgun metagenomes, developed by members of our team and used by research groups worldwide. It turns a tangle of mixed environmental DNA into separated, analyzable genomes.

It is exactly the kind of work we do now for clients: a hard scientific problem, solved as real, reproducible, maintained software rather than a one-off script.
